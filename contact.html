Common Questions

Find quick answers to frequently asked questions about contacting us.

What is your response time for emails?

We strive to respond to all emails within 24 hours during business days (Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 8 PM IST). For urgent matters, please call our customer service number for immediate assistance.

What are your business hours?

Our customer service team is available Monday to Saturday from 9 AM to 8 PM IST. Outside these hours, you can leave us a message or email, and we'll get back to you on the next business day.

What information should I include in my support request?

For faster resolution, please include: your full name, contact information, clear description of your inquiry or issue, and any relevant details. This helps our team understand and address your concern more efficiently.

Do you provide support in regional languages?

Yes, our customer service team supports Hindi, English, and other regional languages. Please let us know your preferred language when you contact us, and we'll do our best to assist you in that language.
